Project Background

The Global Database of Events, Language, and Tone (GDELT) captures news articles from around the world and stores them in a database that can currently be accessed by the public using Google Cloud Services or Amazon Web Services.

I filtered the dataset for topics related to education in order to perform an exploratory analysis of how educational topics are covered within the US media. Some of the questions I was interested in answering include: which states have the greatest media coverage of educational topics, how are laws regarding education received by the public, which states are talking about curriculum the most, amongst others.
